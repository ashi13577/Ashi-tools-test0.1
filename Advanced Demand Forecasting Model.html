Key Performance Metrics

  • MAD (Mean Absolute Deviation): Average absolute forecast error
  • MAPE (Mean Absolute Percentage Error): Percentage-based accuracy measure
  • MSE (Mean Squared Error): Emphasizes larger errors more heavily
  • RMSE (Root Mean Square Error): Standard deviation of forecast errors
  • Tracking Signal: Detects forecast bias over time
