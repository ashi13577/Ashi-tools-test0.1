Go-to-Market Strategy

Outline your plan to deliver your product's unique value proposition to your target customers.

1. Target Audience & Segments

Describe your ideal customers, key demographics, psychographics, and distinct market segments...

2. Value Proposition

What unique value do you offer? What problems do you solve? What makes you different?

3. Product Strategy

Overview of the product/service, key features, benefits, and highlights of the product roadmap...

4. Pricing Strategy

Describe your pricing model (e.g., subscription, freemium, one-time), price points, and any discount or promotional strategies...

5. Marketing & Sales Strategy

How will you reach customers (marketing channels)? How will you sell (sales channels)? What is your core messaging and positioning?

6. Customer Onboarding & Support

Describe the customer onboarding process. How will you provide support and ensure customer success?

7. Success Metrics (KPIs)

List key performance indicators (KPIs) to measure success (e.g., user acquisition, conversion rates, revenue targets)...

8. Budget & Resources

Outline the allocated budget for the GTM strategy and key resources required (team, tools, partnerships)...

9. Timeline / Launch Plan

Key milestones, launch phases, and important dates for the GTM execution...
